Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before starting your concrete project.

Do I need a permit for a new driveway in Miami? −

Yes. Most cities in Miami-Dade require a permit for concrete work. We handle the entire permitting process for you — no stress, no extra cost.

How long until I can drive on new concrete? +

Concrete sets in 24–48 hrs but we recommend waiting 7 days before driving on it for full strength and durability.

What does concrete work cost per square foot? +

It varies based on thickness, finish type, and site prep. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote — usually same day.

Are you licensed and insured in Florida? +

Absolutely. Fully licensed & insured in the State of Florida. Your property and investment are always protected.

Do you do decorative or stamped concrete? +

Yes! We specialize in smooth finishes and premium decorative textures — stamped stone, brick patterns, and custom colors.
